How does the image recognition technology work?

Our project utilizes state-of-the-art image recognition technology powered by deep learning algorithms, particularly the ResNet50 model.

How accurate is the plant identification?

The plant identification process achieves high levels of accuracy, thanks to the robustness of the ResNet50 algorithm employed

Yes, our project is designed to be cross-platform, accessible on both web as well as android.

Absolutely! We encourage users to actively participate in expanding our database by submitting images of medicinal plants encountered in their surroundings.
